Featured Artist

Drake

Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham on October 24, 1986, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and entrepreneur. He first gained recognition for his role as Jimmy Brooks on the television series Degrassi: The Next Generation. Since transitioning to a music career, Drake has become one of the best-selling artists of all time, with numerous chart-topping albums and singles. Known for his versatile style, he has won four Grammy Awards and holds several Billboard chart records. Additionally, Drake has ventured into business, with successful ventures in fashion, whiskey, and technology, solidifying his status as one of the most influential figures in contemporary music and culture.
